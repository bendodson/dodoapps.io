Return to Dark Tower Assistant

Return to Dark Tower Assistant is an iOS / iPadOS app that can keep track of all of your phases, actions, cards, and Advantages in the Return to Dark Tower board game. The app is available for iPhone and iPad on the App Store and contains no advertising, no tracking, and no in-app purchases.

Features

  • Keep track of phases; never forget if you've already reinforced or cleansed this turn
  • Recreate your hero board including virtues, corruptions, treasures, companions, potions, gear, and quest items
  • View all of the available actions from your cards whilst in a specific phase (i.e. "After you Cleanse, remove 1 skull from any building" if you have the Generous virtue)
  • Advantages calculator: easily see how many Advantages you have based on the cards on your hero board
  • Support for 1-4 players including trading of items
  • Progress is automatically saved if you need to break up your play session
  • Optimised for iPad Slide Over so you can use it as an overlay on top of the "Return to Dark Tower" app

Privacy

I believe in your privacy so none of your data ever leaves your device. The app does not require or use an internet connection. There is no advertising or tracking of any kind. You can find out more in my Privacy Policy.

Download

Return to Dark Tower Assistant is available on the App Store and contains no advertising or in-app purchases.

Return to Dark Tower Assistant will run on any iPad, iPhone, or iPod Touch running iOS 15.0 or greater. It is optimised so that it looks great on all sizes of device but I highly recommend you use it in Slide Over mode on an iPad with the "Return to Dark Tower" app running underneath.
