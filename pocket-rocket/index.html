Pocket Rocket

Pocket Rocket is an iOS app that gives you a quick way to view your Pocket articles in Mobile Safari. Simply sign into your Pocket account and your list of articles will be displayed; tapping will take you to Safari (or you can choose to use an in-app browser if you'd prefer). This makes viewing articles much quicker but the benefit is greatest on the iPad where you can run Pocket Rocket side-by-side with Safari for quick reading. Pocket Rocket is available for free on the App Store and contains no advertising or in-app purchases.

Features

  • Quick way to view articles in Safari
  • Choose to open articles in Safari or an In-App Browser. You can also open in Firefox or Chrome if installed.
  • Swipe an article (in list view) or long press an article (in grid view) to archive or delete it
  • Long press an article to present sharing options
  • Universal app for iPhone and iPad
  • Optimised for iPad Pro and split-screen usage
  • Ability to see total count of articles on app icon
  • 3D Touch Quick Action for supported devices

Privacy

I believe in your privacy so none of your data ever leaves your device. I fetch all data directly from Pocket and I don't send this data anywhere else. There is no advertising or tracking of any kind. You can find out more in my Privacy Policy.

Download

Pocket Rocket is available for free on the App Store and contains no advertising or in-app purchases.

Pocket Rocket will run on any iPad, iPhone, or iPod Touch running iOS 9.0 or greater. It is optimised so that it looks great on all sizes of device.
